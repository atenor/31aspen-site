Scope

What shapes the investment.

Pricing evolves when the work moves from visual presentation into deep process logic, technical integrations, and long-term stewardship. These are the primary variables that move a project from one tier to the next:

Brand & Design Readiness

If you come to us with a finalized logo, brand guidelines, and written content, the scope is lighter. If we are collaborating to build your visual identity from scratch or write the copy, the effort increases.

Logic & Complexity

A simple contact form is straightforward. A multi-step onboarding flow that changes based on client answers, calculates a custom quote, and generates a PDF requires serious logic and architectural planning.

Integrations

Securely linking your new system to the tools you already rely on like taking payments through Stripe, syncing with a specific CRM, or pulling data from an external database adds implementation and testing time.

Stewardship

Some clients need a project delivered and handed off. Others need a system that continues evolving, expanding, and getting refined after launch.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to buy my own domain, hosting, and email first?

No. If you already have them, we can seamlessly take over the management or integrate with your current setup. If you are starting from scratch, our Managed Infrastructure tier handles everything from registering your domain to configuring your professional email.

Do all projects fit neatly into one package?

No. These are reference points designed to give you clarity. Your business is unique, some work crosses multiple categories, and larger builds are often delivered in phases.

Can we start with a website and expand into automations later?

Absolutely, and we often recommend it. Establishing a sharp, high-performing public website is the perfect foundation. Once that is in place, we can seamlessly add internal dashboards, portals, or complex automations as phase two.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Yes. Every build starts with 30 to 90 days of included white-glove support to ensure a perfectly smooth rollout. After that, we offer Managed Infrastructure and Monthly Maintenance plans so we can act as your complete outsourced technical team to handle updates, troubleshooting, and continuous improvements.
