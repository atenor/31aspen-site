31 Aspen Holdings is a privately held company focused on curating and investing in a disciplined portfolio of innovative and groundbreaking businesses across creative, technical, and service-driven fields.

Each business within the portfolio operates with autonomy while benefiting from shared perspective and structural support. Like the aspen groves that blanket the Rocky Mountain landscape, our strength lies in deep roots and interconnected growth.

We maintain a long-view perspective, emphasizing thoughtful growth, independent leadership, and strategic alignment across our companies.
