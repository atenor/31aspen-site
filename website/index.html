About

Building ventures with
lasting potential

31 Aspen Holdings is a privately held company that develops, operates, and invests in a growing portfolio of independent ventures across creative, technology, and service-driven sectors.

The company functions both as a holding structure and a venture builder, launching new platforms while providing strategic oversight, operational support, and shared infrastructure across its portfolio.

Each venture operates with independence while benefiting from a common foundation of leadership, technical development, and long-term strategic alignment.

Our focus is on building scalable platforms and durable businesses designed to grow, evolve, and create lasting value.

The name 31 Aspen reflects the structure of aspen groves in nature, where individual trees grow independently above ground while remaining connected through a shared root system beneath the surface. In much the same way, each venture within 31 Aspen Holdings stands on its own while benefiting from the strength and perspective of the broader network.
